Questions

What materials do you use to make your custom dice?

We use high-quality materials such as resin, acrylic, and metal to craft our custom dice. We ensure durability and aesthetic appeal in every set we create.

Can I request specific designs or symbols on my custom dice?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating unique and personalized designs. You can provide us with your preferred symbols, images, or even custom artwork, and we will incorporate them into your custom dice.

Can I order custom dice in different quantities, or do I have to purchase them in bulk?

We cater to both individual customers and bulk orders. Whether you need a single set of custom dice for personal use or want to order a larger quantity for events or promotions, we can accommodate your needs. Simply let us know your desired quantity, and we will provide you with a competitive quote.

Do you offer any warranties or guarantees on your custom dice?

We take pride in the quality of our custom dice and offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you encounter any issues or defects with your order, please reach out to us, and we will work with you to find a suitable solution, which may include replacement or refund options. Customer satisfaction is our top priority
